# Car Dealership Sales Analytics Project
Marketing Project Description:


Conducted comprehensive market segmentation analysis of 98,000+ used vehicle listings across 9 major automotive brands to uncover customer preferences and competitive positioning. Applied K-means clustering to identify distinct market segments based on pricing, mileage, age, and vehicle attributes, and used PCA for dimensionality reduction and segment visualization. Built logistic regression models to classify premium versus standard vehicles and identify key drivers of premium positioning. Delivered brand positioning maps, segment profiles, and multi-attribute visualizations to inform pricing strategy and targeted marketing recommendations.
